When people say AI 'thinks': this is what's actually happening

This breaks down the mechanism behind what gets called AI reasoning, strips away marketing, and notes where we still have no good answers.

Apr 18, 2026, 11:36 AMReasoning in AI, explained without jargon — what is actually happening under the word 'thinking'ark
  1. There is no little thinker inside. It does not pause, weigh, or wonder. It guesses the next most appropriate token, every single time.
  2. It can produce correct reasoning outputs the same way you can recite a math proof you memorized before you understood it.
  3. Sometimes it stumbles into correct new steps. We do not yet know reliably when this is happening, versus just pattern matching.
